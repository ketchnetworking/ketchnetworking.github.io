Optical LAN Training: Network Planning & Design

Do you need to plan and design Optical LAN solutions?

This course is designed for network engineers who are, or will be, responsible for designing and planning networks for the Tellabs Optical LAN. The curriculum is appropriate for Network Architects, Network Design Consultants and Technical Sales Staff

Class Specifics

Course Duration: 3 days

Class Location: On-Site at Customer Provided Location

Class Size: Up to 8 Students

Day 1 – Optical LAN System Architecture and Applications

Overview

This instructor-led course provides an overview of the Tellabs Optical LAN system. It is an introduction to the network architecture and applications.

After a brief review of the features and functionality of the Tellabs Optical LAN solution, students will learn about the applicable technology, architecture, and hardware functionality.

Course Objectives

Upon completion of this course, students will be able to: 1. Identify the features and functionality of the Tellabs Optical LAN Solution 2. Describe the network functionality of the Tellabs Optical LAN Solution 3. Describe the common equipment associated with the Tellabs Optical LAN Solution equipment series

Day 2-3 – Optical LAN Network Planning & Design

Overview

Day 2 of instruction will provide the knowledge and skills needed to plan and design a network for a Tellabs Optical LAN system.

Students will learn how to design the system to provide the data, voice and IP video services and applications supported by the Optical LAN. The course is designed to help students learn the concepts that have been presented and reinforce what they have learned.

Course Objectives

Upon completion of this course, students will be able to:

  1. Establish a layer one plan based on customer floor plan and drop locations
  2. Prescribe for the customer the proper number and types of OLT shelves and plug in units based on requirements
  3. Establish EMS requirements and configuration
  4. Determine the proper service and uplink provisioning that will be needed to meet the customer needs
