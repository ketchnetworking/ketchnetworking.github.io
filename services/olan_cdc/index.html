Optical LAN Training: Deployment Certification

Are you a Tellabs OLAN Reseller that needs to certify installations?

This course is designed for Network Operations and/or any individuals responsible for installing, operating, provisioning, maintaining and monitoring a Tellabs Optical LAN system. The curriculum is specifically designed for the following roles who have little to no prior experience installing, turning up, operating and provisioning a Tellabs Optical LAN system.

This course must be conducted at Tellabs facility in Dallas, TX or suitable lab environment to support extensive hands on activities.

Prerequisites

Prior to taking this certification program, it is required that students have fundamental knowledge of the following areas:

  • Ethernet switching and routing, VLANs, IP sub-netting and basic Port security concepts
  • Public switched telephone network experience
  • Installation Fundamentals
  • Fiber Optic fundamentals

Target Audience

  • Deployment Technicians
  • Support Technicians
  • Maintenance Technicians

Class Specifics

Course Duration: 5 days

Class Location: Contact us for details

Class Size: 4-8 Students

Equipment Ratio: 2:1 student to equipment ratio

This class is 75% hands on activity.

Day 1 – Optical LAN System Architecture and Applications

Overview

This instructor-led course provides an overview of the Tellabs Optical LAN system. It is an introduction to the network architecture and applications.

After a brief review of the features and functionality of the Tellabs Optical LAN solution, students will learn about the applicable technology, architecture, and hardware functionality.

Course Objectives

Upon completion of this course, students will be able to:

  1. Identify the features and functionality of the Tellabs Optical LAN Solution
  2. Describe the network functionality of the Tellabs Optical LAN Solution
  3. Describe the common equipment associated with the Tellabs Optical LAN Solution equipment series

Day 2 – Optical LAN Installation Practices

Day 2 of the Optical LAN Deployment Certification course provides the student with the knowledge and skills necessary to install, turn up and test the Optical LAN hardware.

Course Objectives

After taking this course the students will be able to:

  1. Discuss installation and test procedures for Tellabs Optical LAN power and shelving equipment
  2. Turn Up the Tellabs Optical LAN shelf and establish network connectivity

Day 3-4 – Optical LAN Operations, Provisioning and Maintenance

Overview

The instructor-led course provides knowledge and skills needed to operate, provisioning, and maintain a Tellabs Optical LAN system using the Tellabs Panorama PON Element Management System (EMS).

After a brief introduction to the Tellabs Optical LAN system and hardware functionality, students will learn how to provision the system to provide the voice, data, and video services and application supported by the Tellabs Optical LAN system.

This course includes hands on activities using Tellabs training lab equipment. It is designed to help students learn the concepts that have been presented and reinforce what they have learned.

Course Objectives

Upon completion of this course, students will be able to:

  1. Perform provisioning procedures for the Tellabs Optical LAN voice and data services using Panorama PON.
  2. Perform equipment management and monitoring procedures using the Tellabs Panorama PON EMS
  3. Perform trouble clearing procedures of common problems

Day 5 – Hands on Lab Activities

Certification Testing (SL40-ODTEST)

A test voucher will be delivered to students who complete the Optical LAN Deployment Certification class (when purchased.) Students may schedule their certification test any time following class. Tellabs will receive test scores to update student records following test completion. Certifications are valid for a period of 2 years following a passing test.
