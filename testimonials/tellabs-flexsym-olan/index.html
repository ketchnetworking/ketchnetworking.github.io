Tellabs Optical LAN

Tellabs FlexSym Series, a symmetrical 10 gigabit XGS-PON solution that provides significantly more flexibility to design an enterprise Local Area Network (LAN) for both commercial and federal government customers. The Tellabs FlexSym solution provides the unique ability to serve both 10G and G-PON on the same system, and even on the same PON service card, providing infinite options and a graceful migration to future higher speed network needs.

  • Select XFP-based 10G XGS-PON and 2.5G G-PON at OLT
  • Choose G-PON ONTs and XGS-PON ONTs where needed
  • Mount ONTs in telecom closets and extend ONTs deep in buildings
  • Optical distribution network split ratio can be up to 64 wide splits
  • Single or dual feed Type-B PON protection for fiber route diversity
  • Extend 1G, 2.5G, 5G and 10G Ethernet speeds from the ONTs
  • Built-in reserve capacity to grow network density, and speeds
  • Software Defined LAN functionality today
  • Path to 100G network uplink and 40 gigabit NG-PON2 for the future
