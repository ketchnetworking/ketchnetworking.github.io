Tellabs 1000 Multi-Service Access Platform

Service providers face three conflicting challenges when it comes to maintaining and upgrading their access networks.

They must:

  • Satisfy the demand for TDM-based business services, even while transitioning to an all-IP Ethernet-centric network.
  • Keep delivering legacy services, despite the fact that many legacy equipment vendors have Manufacture Discontinued (MD’d) their product lines.
  • Provide the most cost effective infrastructure while providing a variety of diseperate and technologically diverse services.
